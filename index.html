About Me

Eva is a bilingual Producer and Screenwriter with bases in both Toronto and Montreal. She graduated from Humber’s Television Writing and Producing graduate program with honours, to seek out a career in storytelling and in the television world! Eva has experience working in a writer’s rooms, reading scripts and giving industry level notes to writers and authors alike. Currently, she Produces the weekly radio show and podcast “Finding Your Bliss” on Zoomer Radio AM740 FM96.7. In addition, Eva is writing and developing a Medical Drama Pilot and adapting the novel “The Most Amazing Department Store” with the original author, Sharon Neiss-Arbess, for the big screen! Her work also extends to many short films, including “Julian and the Wind,” which premiered at TIFF in 2025, directed by acclaimed actor-director Conor Jessup. Eva loves meeting and collaborating with other ambitious creatives with fascinating stories to tell!
